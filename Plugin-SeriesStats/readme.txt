SeriesStats for Hattrick Organizer
-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-


Description and information
---------------------------
SeriesStats uses HOs database to display the development in your league!

On the gerneal panel you can see a graphical preparation of every team like HOs 
matches statistic. On the rating panel you can compare the ratings of different
matches and finally on the division panel you can see a graphical average of the
league.
